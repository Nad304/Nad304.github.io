What is Nanotechnology Engineering? you may ask...

It is the study of how to utilize and manipulate materials and devices in the nanoscale

(how cool is that!). Think of it as a mixture of Chemical, Electrical and

Mechanical Engineering but in the 10^-9 scale!

and UW Orbital design team as a payload sub-team member.
